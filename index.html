About Us

Matthew Pockrus is the owner and sole technician at Bachmobile German Auto Service. He has spent years developing deep expertise in German vehicles and the unique engineering, electronics, and performance systems that set them apart.

When you bring your car to Bachmobile, Matthew is the person working on it — every time. There are no other technicians on staff, which means your vehicle always gets the owner's full attention and skill.

The shop is located on FM 2920 in Tomball and serves drivers throughout the greater Houston area. Bachmobile was founded on a straightforward idea: German car owners deserve a specialist, not a generic shop that works on everything.

Customers consistently describe Matthew as knowledgeable, honest, and fairly priced — qualities that are harder to find than they should be in auto repair.
